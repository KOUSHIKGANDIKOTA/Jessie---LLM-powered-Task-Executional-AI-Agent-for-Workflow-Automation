Here is a sample leave letter:

Dear Prasad,

I am writing to inform you that I will be taking a leave of absence from [date] to [date]. I have a personal matter that I need to attend to and I will be unavailable during this time.

I will ensure that all my tasks are completed and my work is up to date before I leave. If there are any urgent matters that need my attention while I am away, please reach out to [alternative contact person's name].

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your understanding in this matter. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to me.

Thank you for your support and I look forward to catching up with you upon my return.

Sincerely,
Koushik
